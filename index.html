Our Impact

01 Donations to school children

We Have Helped Over 700 Children To Stay In School And Learn Effectively By Donating Educational Materials (Including Exercice Books, Pencils, Text Books, Erasers) School Bags, School Uniforms, Shoes, Clothing In Domangburi, Gindabor And Surrounding Communities.
Through our Menstrual Health Programs and collaborations we have donated over 750 Sanitary Pads to young girls in the Upper West Region and Savannah Region.

02 Capacity Building Training

Our Programs have empowered over 1000 young girls and women in the Savannah Region, Northern Region and the Upper West Region. Our programs provided capacity building and free skill training in Soap, Pomade and bead making

03 Female Leadership

Over 1000 Young Girls And Women Have Received Leadership Training And Mentorship From Porducare Touchlife Making Them Poised To Lead Themselves As Well As Take Up Leadership Roles In Their Communities.

Recent Development

In partnership with Sisters Keepers, we implemented the B-GILD project in the Savannah Region, bringing together young girls to inspire leadership and community action. The girls were guided to choose local issues as personal projects, equipped with mentorship, and engaged in a transformative summer camp in Accra. They received support from professionals and were awarded cash prizes to help complete their projects.
Currently, these girls are receiving ongoing sponsorship as they work to finalize and implement their initiatives, creating meaningful change in their communities.
