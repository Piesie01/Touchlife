Our Journey

In a world where three square meals a day is impossible for some household, it is unimaginable what some go through to afford basic needs like food, shelter and clothing for their families and dependants. Young people living with dreams shattered with little or no opportunity to access quality education, better living standard and empowered future. This among others has force many young people who would have contributed their quota into making the world a better place, engage in social vices, just to make ends meet.
It is against this backdrop that the Porducare Touchlife was founded, to help solve the aforementioned problem in Northern Ghana. Porducare Touchlife is a Non Governmental Organisation, registered under Porducare Enterprise with a focal aim to partner with individuals and other organisations to implement community development initiatives through education, Empowering women into leadership positions, skill training, donations among others.

  • Our Mission Statement

    To promote quality education, empower women and fostering leadership in the communities we serve.

  • Our Vision Statement

    To create an enabling community where every individual has access to quality education, women are empowered to achieve their full potentials, and female leadership accelerated and enhanced.
